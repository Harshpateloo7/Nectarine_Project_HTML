Nectarine

Description

Nectarine is variety of peach. Peaches and nectarines are the same species even though they
are sold commercially as different fruits. The species namepersica refers to it's original
location in Persia.It was moved from there to Europe. A domesticated peach appeared in Japan in
4700-4400 BC. It appeared in India by 1700 BC and in Greece by 300BC. Some sources
claim that Alexander the Great introduced the peach to Europ.(source:Wikipedia1)
